International Day of Plant Health - May 12th

The International Day of Plant Health is observed annually on May 12th. It's a day dedicated to raising awareness about the importance of plant health for food security, environmental sustainability, and economic development.

Plants are essential for life on Earth, providing food, oxygen, and habitats for wildlife. International Day of Plant Health aims to highlight the threats posed by pests, diseases, and invasive species to plant health and promote measures to protect plant biodiversity and ecosystems.
